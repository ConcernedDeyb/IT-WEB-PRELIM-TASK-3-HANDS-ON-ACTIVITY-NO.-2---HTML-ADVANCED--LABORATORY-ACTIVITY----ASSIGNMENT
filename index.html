Event Overview ★

SMCC-CCIS Week 2025 is annual campus event that highlights student projects, industry talks, and hands-on workshops about software development, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and more.

Join us for a week of learning, networking, and fun ♥ as we celebrate the power of technology in education and innovation.

HTML vs XHTML

Compare the following snippets:


<!-- HTML style (not self-close) -->
<br>
<img src="images/logo.png" alt="Event Logo">

<!-- XHTML style (self-closed, lowercase, quoted attributes) -->
<br />
<img src="images/logo.png" alt="Event Logo" />

XHTML requires properly closed tags, lowercase element names, and quoted attributes.
